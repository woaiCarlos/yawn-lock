# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

## Our Pledge / 我们的承诺

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:
- Age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality
- Personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation

我们承诺让每个人都能在不受骚扰的环境下参与本项目,无论其:
- 年龄、体型、残疾、族裔、性别特征、性别认同和表达
- 经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍
- 个人外貌、种族、宗教或性认同和取向

## Our Standards / 我们的标准

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

有助于营造积极环境的行为示例:
- 使用友好和包容的语言
- 尊重不同的观点和经验
- 优雅地接受建设性批评
- 关注对社区最有利的事
- 对其他社区成员表现出同理心

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

不可接受的行为示例:
- 使用性化语言或图像、不受欢迎的性关注或调情
- 钓鱼、侮辱/贬损性评论、人身或政治攻击
- 公开或私下骚扰
- 未经明确许可发布他人私人信息
- 在专业环境中可被合理认为不当的其他行为

## Enforcement / 执行

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct. They may take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

项目维护者有责任澄清和执行本行为准则。他们可能对任何他们认为不当、威胁、冒犯或有害的行为采取适当和公平的纠正措施。

## Reporting / 报告

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

不当、骚扰或其他不可接受行为的实例可以通过联系项目团队报告。所有投诉都将被审查和调查,并根据情况做出必要和适当的回应。
